# Data_projects_TripleTen
BIA TripleTen projects by Jeremiah.

| Project number | Title | Description |
| :-----------: | ----------- |----------- |
| 1 | [BIA Data Jam](https://github.com/lookitsjerry/Data_projects_TripleTen/tree/main/BI%20Data%20Jam) | Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to visualize New York City air quality data, specifically focusing on the intersection of environmental equity and public health. I transformed complex datasets into spatial and temporal visualizations to identify neighborhood-level pollution disparities and their correlation with local health outcomes. |
| 2 | [Airbnb Data Analysis](https://github.com/lookitsjerry/Data_projects_TripleTen/tree/main/Spreadsheet%20Data%20Analysis) | Leveraged Excel to perform a quantitative analysis of Manhattan housing data, focusing on varioius metrics and neighborhood availability. I utilized advanced Pivot Tables and lookup functions to compare property sizes and pricing trends across various neighborhoods, providing a data-driven roadmap for their home search. |
| 3 | [Business Analytics Project](https://github.com/lookitsjerry/Data_projects_TripleTen/tree/main/Business%20Analytics%20Project) | Engineered a data pipeline in Microsoft Excel to transform over 4,000 rows of raw e-commerce transaction logs into high-level business intelligence. By performing complex data cleaning and utilizing advanced Pivot Tables, I identified critical patterns in customer behavior, conversion rates, and retention trends to provide actionable insights for the executive team. |
| 4 | [Superstore Tableau Project](https://github.com/lookitsjerry/Data_projects_TripleTen/tree/main/Superstore%20Tableau%20Project) | Utilized Tableau to perform a turnaround analysis on Superstore operational data. Engineered interactive visualizations to pinpoint inefficiencies in shipping modes, regional product demand, and return rates, resulting in actionable recommendations to optimize inventory and restore corporate profitability. |
| 5 | [Shopify Power BI Project](https://github.com/lookitsjerry/Data_projects_TripleTen/tree/main/Shopify%20Project) | Developed a multi-page PowerBI dashboard to analyze a dataset of scraped Shopify app store data. I utilized advanced DAX to create weighted success metrics and established complex table relationships to evaluate developer performance. The project focused on identifying high-growth opportunities through trend analysis and sentiment modeling. |
